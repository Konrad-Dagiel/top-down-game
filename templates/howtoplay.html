How To Play

Controls

MOVE

Move speed varies with Character

SHOOT

Weapon type varies with Character

RELOAD

Some Characters (eg. GRIM) have one-shot weapons, hence no reload

ABILITY

Can only be used after a short cooldown

ULTIMATE

Can only be used when charged to 100%, charge by damaging enemies

Goal

Story Mode:

  • Run away from the dragon through different terrains, fighting his minions at each new land!
  • Defeat the dragon once you reach his cave!
  • If your hp reaches 0, you die!

Endless Mode:

  • Kill all enemies to proceed forward
  • Survive as long as possible agains endless waves of enemies
  • How many levels can you survive?
  • If logged in, highscore will be tracked
  • The top 10 high scores are also tracked and viewable HERE

ENEMIES:

  • Zombie
    • Moves slowly and unintelligently.
    • Easy to kill, does small damage if touching player
    • No special effect
  • Baphomet
    • Moves semi-intelligently
    • Hard to kill, does double damage if touching player
    • Will charge 3 times at random intervals
    • Charge direction is dictated by players past position, so keep moving!
  • Dragon
    • Will perform a more accurate version of Baphomet's charge
    • Can spawn up to 14 other creatures
    • Good luck killing this beast!
